Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 11, 2026

1. Who We Are

CPO Leads is operated by StrategioAI ("we", "us", "our"). We provide a job aggregation platform for close protection and security professionals at cpoleads.com.

2. What We Collect

  • Account data: Email address and password when you sign up (stored securely via Supabase Auth).
  • Payment data: Processed entirely by Stripe. We never see or store your card number.
  • Usage data: Pages visited, features used, saved jobs. Used to improve the platform.
  • Contact form: Name, email, and message when you contact us.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Provide access to the platform and job listings.
  • Process your subscription via Stripe.
  • Respond to your inquiries.
  • Improve the platform based on usage patterns.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on Supabase (hosted in AWS EU/US regions) with row-level security enabled. Payments are handled by Stripe, a PCI DSS Level 1 certified payment processor. All data transmission uses HTTPS/TLS encryption.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase: Authentication and database (supabase.com/privacy).
  • Stripe: Payment processing (stripe.com/privacy).
  • Vercel: Hosting (vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy).

6. Your Rights

You can:

  • Request a copy of your data.
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data.
  • Cancel your subscription at any time via Stripe.

To exercise these rights, email strategioai@strategioai.com.

7. Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication (Supabase session). We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics.

8. Data Retention

Account data is retained while your account is active. Upon deletion request, all personal data is removed within 30 days. Contact form submissions are retained for 12 months.

9. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email strategioai@strategioai.com.
