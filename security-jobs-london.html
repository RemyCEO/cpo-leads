Close Protection Jobs in London

London is Europe's largest market for close protection professionals. The city's concentration of high-net-worth individuals, international diplomats, royal families, and corporate headquarters creates consistent demand for qualified CPOs.

Key areas for CP work include Mayfair, Knightsbridge, Belgravia, Kensington, and The City. Many roles involve protecting Middle Eastern, Russian, and Asian principals with London residences.

London Security Job Types

Residential Security (RST)

Protecting HNW family homes in Mayfair, Knightsbridge, and surrounding areas. 12-hour shifts, often permanent contracts. 150-250 GBP/day.

Mobile Close Protection

Accompanying principals around London and on UK/international travel. Requires SIA licence, clean driving licence, and professional appearance. 200-400 GBP/day.

Corporate EP

Protecting C-suite executives at offices, events, and during travel. Often through major security firms like G4S, Securitas, or specialist CP companies.

Event Security

VIP protection at London events, galas, and private functions. Short-term contracts with good networking opportunities.

London CP Day Rates

  • RST / Static: 150-250 GBP/day
  • Mobile CP: 200-400 GBP/day
  • Team Leader: 300-500 GBP/day
  • International travel (from London): 350-600 GBP/day

Requirements for London Security Jobs

  • SIA Close Protection Licence — mandatory for all CP work in the UK
  • First Aid (FREC 3) — required by most employers
  • DBS Enhanced Check — clean criminal record
  • EVOC Driving Certificate — essential for mobile CP roles
  • Valid UK Driving Licence — clean, full licence
  • Right to Work in UK — British/Irish passport or valid work visa
