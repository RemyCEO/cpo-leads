FAQ

Common questions

Who is CPO Leads for?
CPO Leads is built for close protection operators, executive protection agents, and security professionals looking for contract or full-time work internationally. Whether you're experienced or transitioning from military/law enforcement, the platform gives you the tools and intel to find work faster.
How often are jobs updated?
Job listings are refreshed daily. New positions are added as they appear, and expired listings are removed to keep the database current and actionable. The company database is also continuously updated with new firms and intel.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. There are no contracts or lock-in periods. Cancel your subscription at any time and you'll retain access until the end of your current billing period. No questions asked.
Do you cover my region?
We cover the major CP markets globally: UK, USA, UAE and the Gulf, Europe, Africa, Asia-Pacific, and Latin America. Our database includes companies operating across multiple regions, so even niche markets are represented. If you don't see your region, contact us — we're expanding constantly.
Is my data private?
Absolutely. Your account data, application tracking, and usage are private and encrypted. We do not share your information with any third parties, including the companies in our database. Your job search is your business.
