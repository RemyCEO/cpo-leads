Frequently Asked Questions

CPO LEADS is built exclusively for close protection operatives, security consultants, maritime security officers, PSD team leaders, and other professionals in the global security and defense industry. Whether you are ex-military, law enforcement, or a seasoned private contractor, the platform connects you with verified employers and high-value contracts worldwide.
Every employer on CPO LEADS undergoes a rigorous vetting process. We verify company registration, operational history, insurance status, and client references. Only employers meeting our strict standards gain access to the operator pool. This protects you from fraudulent postings and ensures every contract is legitimate.
CPO LEADS employs military-grade encryption across all data transmission and storage. Your personal information, deployment history, and communications are protected with end-to-end encryption. We follow zero-knowledge architecture for messaging, meaning not even our team can read your messages. All infrastructure is hosted in SOC 2 compliant data centers.

High-risk deployments in conflict zones command premium rates, while executive protection in lower-risk environments will differ accordingly. Each listing includes compensation details so you can filter opportunities that match your expectations.
